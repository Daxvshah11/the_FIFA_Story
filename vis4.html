Inference

Zoomable Packing is a visualisation that talks about heirarchy as well as value difference about a particular comparable & measurable attribute. Here, the packing is about all the countries that have ever won the FIFA and it helps in understanding that the winners are more associated with only 2 continents overall ie. Europe & South America

Further, on clicking on each continent, we are revealed to the sizes of each of the countries in those continents that are contributing to the continent's representation in FIFA wins. Those different size show the relative number of cups won by those countries eg. Italy & Germany being of same size in Europe show clearly that they have won total of smae number of cups.
